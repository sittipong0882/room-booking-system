Looney tunes


"Looney Tunes" is a cartoon franchise created by Warner Bros. It is one of the cartoon franchises that have important and very impact in the entertainment industry. It is one of the longest cartoons in the industry. This is a short history of "Looney Tunes" Beginning in 1930s "Looney Tunes" began in 1930 by producing "Sinkin 'in the Bathub", the first cartoon of the franchise. Which was first published on May 19, 1930. Golden Age of Animation: In the Golden Age of Animation in the 190s to 1950s, "Looney Tunes" became the number one in the most popular cartoon. During this period, there are famous works such as Tex Avery, Chuck Jones, FRIZ FRELENG, and Robert McCimson working with "Looney Tunes." Golden Age: After the Golden Age, "Looney Tunes" has been resurrected in many forms, including movies, TV shows, and new cartoon production. "Looney Tunes" is well -known throughout the world and has a huge cultural impact in the entertainment industry. The characters and cartoons of "Looney Tunes" are still popular today and have an impact on the entertainment industry in terms of art, entertainment, and products.
